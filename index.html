CSS Grid Layout System

Grid with Three Columns Structure

1 → col-span-3 (takes full row)
2 → row-span-2 (takes 2 rows height)
3 → col-span-2 (takes 2 columns)
4 → normal (1 col, 1 row)
5 → normal
6 → full row again 

<div class="bg-green-300 max-w-xl w-full mx-auto p-4 grid grid-cols-3 gap-2">
<div class="bg-green-700 text-white p-2 col-span-3">1 → col-span-3 (takes full row)</div>
<div class="bg-green-700 text-white p-2 row-span-2 flex items-center justify-center">2 → row-span-2 (takes 2 rows height)</div>
<div class="bg-green-700 text-white p-2 col-span-2">3 → col-span-2 (takes 2 columns)</div>
<div class="bg-green-700 text-white p-2">4 → normal (1 col, 1 row)</div>
<div class="bg-green-700 text-white p-2">5 → normal</div>
<div class="bg-green-700 text-white p-2 col-span-3">6 → full row again</div></div>
